3. Camouflage: Another reason chameleons change colors is for camouflage purposes. By changing the color of their skin, they can blend in with their surroundings and avoid predators or prey.
4. Mood changes: Chameleons also use color changes to express their mood. For example, a bright red color may indicate aggression or excitement, while a pale color may suggest fear or submission.
