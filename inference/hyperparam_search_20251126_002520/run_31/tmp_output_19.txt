The benefits of eating apples are numerous. For example:
1. Rich in Fiber: One medium-sized apple contains about 4 grams of fiber, which helps keep you feeling full for longer periods and reduces your risk of developing type 2 diabetes and heart disease.
2. High in Antioxidants: Apples contain antioxidants like quercetin and catechins that help fight free radicals and reduce the risk of cancer, Alzheimer's disease, and other age-related diseases.
3. Low in Calories: An apple is a great snack option for those watching their calorie intake because it contains only about 95 calories per medium-sized fruit.
4. Good Source of Vitamin C: Apples are an excellent source of vitamin C, which helps boost your immune system and support healthy skin and hair growth.
