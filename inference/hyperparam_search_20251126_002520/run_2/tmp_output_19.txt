Apples contain several vitamins including vitamin C which helps boost your immune system. They also provide fiber which can help with digestion and weight loss. Eating an apple a day has even been linked to lower cholesterol levels and reduced risk of heart disease.
In addition to these physical benefits, apples are great for mental health as well. The antioxidants in apples have anti-inflammatory properties that may reduce the symptoms of depression and anxiety. Apples also contain serotonin which is often called "the happy hormone" because it helps regulate mood.
