Fortune cookies are small round biscuits filled with paper-thin sheets of almond or peanut butter coated in sugar syrup. They typically contain one piece of advice, a quote from literature or philosophy, or an inspirational message written on the inside sheet of cookie dough before baking.
Today, fortune cookies have become popular around the world and can be found at many restaurants, bakeries, cafes, and events such as weddings, birthdays, graduations, and holidays. They are often given to guests as a token of good luck or positive energy for their day ahead.
