

An apple a day is often recommended as part of a healthy diet. There are many benefits to eating apples regularly, including:

  * They are high in fiber and can help with digestion.
  * Apples contain antioxidants that may help reduce the risk of certain diseases.
  * Eating an apple before bed can promote better sleep due to its natural sugar content.
  * Apples are a good source of vitamin C and other important nutrients.

Overall, eating apples regularly as part of a healthy diet can provide many benefits for your health. [end of text]
