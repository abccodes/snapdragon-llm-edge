Georgia's climate and soil conditions are ideal for growing peaches, which thrive in warm temperatures and well-drained sandy loam. Additionally, the state has invested heavily in modern agriculture technology to improve yields and reduce costs, further contributing to its status as one of the leading producers of peaches in the United States.
