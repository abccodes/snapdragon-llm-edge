That line becomes one of Disney’s most recognizable quotes from its earliest animated feature.

However it should be noted that these words are not in fact spoken by Queen Elsa (played by Mary Poppins) but rather a character named Snow White played by Lillian Hellman. 

Queen Elsa doesn’t actually speak at all, she is silent throughout the film and only communicates through song or gestures.

So what about mirror? The exact quote isn't "Mirror Mirror on wall" as it's often remembered in popular culture. That line comes from a different Disney movie - Beauty and the Beast. In Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs, Queen Elsa uses these words to communicate with her magic mirror which is actually an enchanted glass ball.

The full dialogue goes: 

Queen Elsa (in German): "Was ist am schönsten?" 
Mirror responds in English as it's a magical object that speaks:

"Ich sehe ein wunderschones Kind."  
Which translates back into English:

"There I see such beautiful child."
