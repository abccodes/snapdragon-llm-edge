The World Health Organization states "There is no evidence to suggest that MSG itself or any reasonable amount used as seasoning in foods could cause serious harm." The United States Food & Drug Administration (FDA) also considers MSG safe when consumed by people of all ages. 

However, some studies have shown link between excessive consumption and certain health issues such as headache , nausea , vomiting etc.

It is advised to consume MSG only in moderation for those who are sensitive or allergic towards it.
In conclusion there's no scientific proof that MSG itself can cause serious harm when consumed by people of all ages. However caution should be exercised while consuming too much amount which may lead to some adverse effects like headache, nausea and vomiting etc.

It is always advisable to consult with a medical professional if you have any concerns about the safety or potential side-effects associated with food ingredients such as MSG.
