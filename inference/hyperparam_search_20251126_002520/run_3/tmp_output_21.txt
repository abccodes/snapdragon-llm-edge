This question is related to the classic Disney animated film Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs, released in 1937. The Queen's question to her mirror is a famous line from the movie and has become a cultural reference. In the film, the Queen is jealous of Snow White's beauty and asks the mirror who is the fairest of them all, to which the mirror responds that Snow White is the fairest. This leads to the Queen's downfall in the story.
