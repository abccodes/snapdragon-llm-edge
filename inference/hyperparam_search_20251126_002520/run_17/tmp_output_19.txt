2. Apples contain antioxidants: Antioxidants help protect against cell damage caused by free radicals.
3. Apples may lower the risk of heart disease: Eating apples regularly has been linked to a reduced risk of developing heart disease.
4. Apples can aid in weight loss: The fiber content in apples helps you feel full, which can prevent overeating and contribute to weight loss.
5. Apples are high in vitamin C: Vitamin C is an important nutrient that supports the immune system and aids in wound healing.
