 is a question about history. To answer this, we need to know when the United States declared its independence from Great Britain and which document outlines this declaration.

The correct answer is: July 4th, 1776. This day is celebrated as Independence Day in the United States and marks the date on which the Declaration of Independence was signed by representatives from each state. The actual signing took place over several days following a vote for independence on July 2nd. However, July 4th has been commemorated ever since as the official birthday of the nation.
