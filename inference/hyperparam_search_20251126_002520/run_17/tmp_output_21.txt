 

The exact words spoken by the Queen to her mirror in Disney's classic animated film "Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs" from 1937, as she tries to find out if the young princess has grown any taller, are:

"The Evil Queen asks her magic mirror: 'Mirror, Mirror on the wall, who is the fairest of them all?'"

These words become a famous line in popular culture and have been referenced countless times over the years. The scene is one of many that contribute to the iconic story of Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs. [end of text]
