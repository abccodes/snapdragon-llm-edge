So what happens when a mirror breaks? Can we really believe in its superstition?
The truth is rather complicated and depends on how you define "break".
To start off with, there are different types of damage that can be inflicted upon mirrors. The most common type of damage occurs when the frame or backing of the glass shatters due to an impact.
This kind of breakage usually results in a large number of shards scattered all over the place and is considered the "worst" kind of mirror damage, because it means that not only does the broken piece fall on you, but the surrounding pieces also pose a serious danger to your eyes or skin.
