 Fortune cookies are a traditional dessert found in Chinese-American restaurants. They are small, rectangular cookies that are filled with a message or prediction. However, the origin of fortune cookies is not known for certain. Some believe they originated in China, while others believe they originated in Japan. There is also a theory that they originated in the United States, where they were first introduced to Chinese immigrants in the late 19th century. Regardless of their origin, fortune cookies have become an iconic part of Chinese-American cuisine and are enjoyed by people all over the world. [end of text]
