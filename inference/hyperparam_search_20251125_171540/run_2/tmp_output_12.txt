 Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star is an English nursery rhyme. The tune is based on the French song 'Ah, lait de chèvre' ('Milk of the goat') by Jean-Philippe Rameau. This song was originally written in 1731 for a French opera. The English version of the nursery rhyme was first published in 1805. The tune and lyrics were later combined and the result is the tune and lyrics that we know today.
