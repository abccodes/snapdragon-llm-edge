


 Marie Antoinette was famously quoted as saying "Let them eat cake!" during the French Revolution. However, this quote has been proven to be a myth and never actually spoken by her. The real origins of the quote are unknown and may have originated from someone else entirely.

The phrase "let them eat cake" is often used in political discourse today to criticize those who claim they can solve society's problems without facing their own personal costs or sacrifices.
