1. What Happens If You Smash a Mirror?
When you smash a mirror, several things happen:
- Fragments: The glass in the mirror will break and create small shards of glass that can be dangerous to walk on or step over.
- Glass Pieces: The shattered pieces of glass may roll around and cause further damage or injury.
- Clean-up: Cleaning up broken mirrors is usually time-consuming and difficult, as the sharp edges are hazardous to work with. It's best to wear safety gear such as gloves and eye protection when cleaning up a mirror breakage.
2. What Causes Mirrors To Break?
Mirrors can break for several reasons:
- Accidental Impact: The most common cause of broken mirrors is accidental impact or collision. This could be caused by someone stepping on it, dropping something heavy on top of it, or accidentally bumping into it.
- Heat Exposure: If a mirror is exposed to high heat, such as direct sunlight, the glass can become brittle
