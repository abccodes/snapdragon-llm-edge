Apples offer several health benefits including:
1. Reduces risk of heart disease: Eating apples can help reduce the risk of heart disease by lowering cholesterol levels, improving blood pressure and reducing inflammation in the body.
2. Anti-inflammatory properties: Apples contain compounds called polyphenols which have anti-inflammatory properties that can help to reduce pain and swelling in the body.
3. Rich source of fiber: Apples are a good source of fiber which can help promote healthy digestion and prevent constipation.
4. Antioxidant rich: Apples are loaded with antioxidants, such as vitamin C and flavonoids, which can help fight off free radicals that damage cells and contribute to cancer and other chronic diseases.
5. Good for brain health: Eating apples has been linked to improved cognitive function and reduced risk of Alzheimer's disease.
