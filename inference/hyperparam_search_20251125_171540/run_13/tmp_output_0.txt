 This is a question that many people have. In fact, eating watermelon seeds is not as harmful as some people think.
Watermelons are rich in nutrients and benefits human health, including vitamins A, C, E, B1, B2, B6, dietary fiber, calcium, magnesium, potassium, phosphorus, selenium, zinc, copper, etc. The content of these nutrients makes watermelon a superfruit that can help people maintain good health.
Watermelon seeds are rich in minerals and fibers, which not only provide energy but also promote digestive function. They contain plant lignans and phytoestrogens, which have anti-inflammatory effects and may reduce the risk of some diseases such as heart disease, cancer, and diabetes.
However, eating too many watermelon seeds can cause gastrointestinal discomfort and constipation in some people. Therefore, it is recommended to eat them moderately and not overeat.
In summary, eating watermelon seeds has no negative impact on health if consumed in moderation. It is a good choice for those who want to maintain good health and enjoy delicious fruits. [end of text]
