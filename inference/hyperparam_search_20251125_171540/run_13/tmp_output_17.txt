There are several factors to consider when deciding how long to wait between eating a meal and going for a swim:

2. Nutritional needs: Swimmers need fuel to perform well, and consuming food immediately before a swim may not provide enough energy for optimal performance.

3. Individual differences: Some people can tolerate eating closer to swimming without issues, while others may experience digestive problems even with longer gaps between meals and workouts.

In general, it's recommended to wait at least 2-4 hours after eating a meal before going for a swim to allow adequate digestion time. However, if you're feeling hungry or low on energy during your workout, consuming some light snacks such as fruit or nuts may be helpful to maintain fuel levels without causing digestive issues.
