 I asked the receptionist at Disneyland. She replied, "Walt's body can be found in a mausoleum on the north side of Main Street." The grave site has been there for about 40 years now. When Walt Disney died in December 1966, his family chose to give him an honor that most people would find extremely strange - they wanted a private burial at sea. They were worried that if he was buried on land somewhere, people might dig up his body and steal it. So, after receiving permission from the state of California, Walt Disney's casket was taken out to sea in 1967 and dumped about three miles off the coast of Long Beach, California.
