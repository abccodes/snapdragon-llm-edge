1. Temperature regulation: Chameleons use changes in color as a way of regulating their body temperature. When they feel too hot, they will turn darker to absorb more heat from the sun. Conversely, when they feel too cold, they will turn lighter to reflect more sunlight and stay cool.

3. Camouflage: Another reason why chameleons change colors is for camouflage. By blending in with their surroundings, they are better able to avoid predators and find food.
