The question stems from an event known as "the Death of Disneyland". On December 30, 1964, Walt Disney announced his intention to sell Disneyland and retire early. He did not disclose the reason for this decision at the time but later revealed that he was suffering from lung cancer.
On January 27, 1965, Disney suffered a heart attack and died in his sleep at home in Burbank, California. The funeral procession was held on February 3, 1965, with President Lyndon B. Johnson delivering the eulogy.
The question "Where is Walt Disney's body?" arises because Disneyland closed for several days after Disney's death as a sign of respect and to allow guests time to mourn his passing. During this period, many people were curious about where Disney's body was kept.
