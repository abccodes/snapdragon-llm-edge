The primary purpose of Area 51 was to develop advanced military technologies that could give the US a strategic advantage over its adversaries. The facility was used extensively during the Cold War era, when it played a significant role in developing stealth aircraft such as the F-22 Raptor and B-2 Spirit bomber.
