 is an example of what type of logical reasoning?
A. Deductive Reasoning
B. Inductive Reasoning
C. Analogical Reasoning
D. Evaluative Reasoning

The type of logical reasoning being referred to in the question "What was Nixon referring to when he said, I am not a crook? " is:

**Evaluative Reasoning**

In this case, Nixon's statement reflects his evaluation or assessment of himself regarding honesty and integrity. It involves making judgments about his character based on available information or personal beliefs rather than deriving conclusions from premises. Therefore, the correct answer is D: Evaluative Reasoning.

This type of reasoning often includes opinions, evaluations, and assessments rather than logical deductions or inferences drawn from evidence. [end of text]
